Feeding America

President and Chief Executive Officer

Led extensive reorganization and rebranding of the nation’s premier food bank/pantry network not-for-profit, from America’s Second Harvest to Feeding America. Increased donations 300% and doubled the population served by the organization establishing it at the 3rd largest not-for-profit in the nation. Established partnerships with Walmart, JP Morgan Chase, Kraft, Morgan Stanley, General Mills, and other leading companies, quadrupling revenue and helping to grow Feeding America to a $1.2B organization—making it America’s largest domestic hunger relief organization at the time.
